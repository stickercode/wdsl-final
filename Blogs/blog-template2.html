DAVID’S BLOG

Baguio

My trip to Baguio City, the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," was an experience filled with cool mountain breezes, breathtaking views, and cozy moments. As soon as I arrived, the crisp and serene air of the pine trees instantly made me feel at home...

During my stay, I visited Mines View Park and wandered through the local market. Baguio’s charm lies not just in its cold air, but in its people and the culture that makes every visit feel memorable and refreshing.
